# Personalization: Solo coach

## Top 3 connector recommendations (ranked)

1. **Perplexity Ask** — for discovering coaching methodologies, frameworks, and competing-coach positioning
2. **Fathom** — for capturing every coaching call (already installed in most cowork-ai-os installs)
3. **Notion** OR **Obsidian vault** (if cowork-obsidian installed) — for storing frameworks long-term

## Phase 6 demo (first research live-fire)

Run `/research-brief` on a thought-leader the user cited in their `business-brain.md`, OR on the user's top competitor. Output: a 600-word brief showing how their methodology compares.

## Conversation tone for the wizard

Coaches think in transformations and frameworks. Use language like "your client's transformation," "the framework you teach," "how your method compares." Avoid agency/sales jargon.

## Common pitfalls

- Coaches under-research competitors because they think "my work is unique." The wizard should gently push back: even unique work needs context.
- Coaches over-collect frameworks. Phase 4 calibration should default brief length to 600 words (not 1200) to keep things scannable.

## Sample brief topics to suggest in Phase 6

- "Compare my coaching method to [thought-leader they admire]"
- "What does [competitor coach] charge and how do they package?"
- "What's [methodology user uses] and where does it fall short?"

**Quick-capture habit (post-onboarding):** Use `/save-clip` whenever you find an article worth keeping. The library compounds over weeks.
