# Personalization: Solopreneur / small-business owner

## Top 3 connector recommendations (ranked)

1. **Perplexity Ask** — for one-off briefs (the most common solopreneur research need)
2. **Firecrawl** — for occasional CRO checks and competitor scans
3. **Fathom** — for customer-call recall (often the most valuable research input solopreneurs ignore)

## Phase 6 demo (first research live-fire)

Pick based on what's most pressing in `business-brain.md`. If a "current focus" mentions a partner/competitor → run `/research-brief` on them. If it mentions a webpage to fix → run `/web-audit`. If it mentions a client conversation → run `/meeting-recap`.

## Conversation tone for the wizard

Solopreneurs wear every hat. Use language like "this week's most-pressing thing," "the one decision you've been putting off." Avoid specialist jargon.

## Common pitfalls

- Solopreneurs spread research too thin. The wizard should suggest picking ONE rhythm (weekly OR monthly, not both at first).
- Solopreneurs skip the calibration assuming "I'll figure it out." Push back gently — calibration takes 90 seconds.

## Sample research topics to suggest in Phase 6

- "Brief on [a partner the user mentioned]"
- "Audit [the user's homepage]"
- "Recap of [the user's last customer call]"

**Quick-capture habit (post-onboarding):** Use `/save-clip` whenever you find an article worth keeping. The library compounds over weeks.
