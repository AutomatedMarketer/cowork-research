# Personalization: Sales-led B2B

## Top 3 connector recommendations (ranked)

1. **Firecrawl** — pre-call account research is THE job; scraping target accounts at scale
2. **Perplexity Ask** — for prospect/industry context the public web doesn't surface cleanly
3. **Fathom** — post-call action extraction; sales calls are dense and Fathom's transcripts are the ground truth

## Phase 6 demo (first research live-fire)

Run `/research-brief` on a target account from the user's pipeline. Output: 600-word brief on the account's tech stack, recent news, key people, and likely buying triggers.

## Conversation tone for the wizard

Sales-led B2B thinks in accounts, pipeline, and closing. Use language like "your target account," "the next discovery call," "the deal in stage 3." Avoid coach/creator jargon.

## Common pitfalls

- Sales-led B2B over-relies on the CRM and under-uses external research. The wizard should pitch research as "what your CRM doesn't know."
- Sales-led B2B treats `/web-audit` as a prospecting tool, not a competitive tool. Surface both use cases.

## Sample brief topics to suggest in Phase 6

- "Brief on [target account in user's pipeline]"
- "What's [target account] hiring for right now? (signal of priorities)"
- "Pull every news mention of [target account] in the last 90 days"

**Quick-capture habit (post-onboarding):** Use `/save-clip` whenever you find an article worth keeping. The library compounds over weeks.
