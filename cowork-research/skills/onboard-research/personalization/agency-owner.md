# Personalization: Agency owner

## Top 3 connector recommendations (ranked)

1. **Firecrawl** — daily prospect/competitor scraping is the agency-owner job
2. **Perplexity Ask** — for quick context lookups before sales calls
3. **Playwright** — handles login-walled SaaS pages that Firecrawl can't reach

## Phase 6 demo (first research live-fire)

Run `/web-audit` on a recent prospect's homepage. Output: bulleted CRO recommendations the user can send to the prospect as a free-value lead-in.

## Conversation tone for the wizard

Agency owners think in deals and pipeline. Use language like "your prospect," "the next pitch," "the audit you'd send." Avoid coach/creator jargon.

## Common pitfalls

- Agency owners want to skip the calibration phase. The wizard should hold the line — calibration is what makes the audits look like THEIR audits, not generic.
- Agency owners over-rely on Firecrawl and ignore Perplexity. Suggest both as paired tools (scrape + summarize).

## Sample audit URLs to suggest in Phase 6

- The user's own agency homepage (the meta-audit)
- Their top competitor's pricing page
- A recent prospect's homepage (most useful — actionable next-day)

**Quick-capture habit (post-onboarding):** Use `/save-clip` whenever you find an article worth keeping. The library compounds over weeks.
