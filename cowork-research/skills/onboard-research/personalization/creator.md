# Personalization: Creator (content business)

## Top 3 connector recommendations (ranked)

1. **VidIQ** — YouTube/IG competitive intelligence is the creator job; outliers + keyword research is unique signal
2. **Perplexity Ask** — for trend research and current-event context
3. **Firecrawl** — for scraping competitor content pages, sponsorship pages, monetization stacks

## Phase 6 demo (first research live-fire)

Run a VidIQ outliers query against a competitor channel the user named in `business-brain.md`. Output: top 5 outlier videos with view-to-sub ratios.

## Conversation tone for the wizard

Creators think in algorithms, hooks, and content cycles. Use language like "your channel," "the hook," "what's working right now." Avoid coach/agency jargon.

## Common pitfalls

- Creators under-document research because they think it'll slow them down. The wizard should emphasize speed (5-min briefs, not 30-min essays).
- Creators over-focus on YouTube and ignore Perplexity for trend context. Suggest pairing.

## Sample research topics to suggest in Phase 6

- "What's working on [competitor channel] in the last 30 days?"
- "Pull the top 5 outliers from [competitor] and tell me their hooks"
- "What hooks are trending in [user's niche] this week?"
